BSD coreutils for arch

Tired of the bloaty-ness and unposix-ness of GNU coreutils? Wondering why echo.c has 270 lines and everything but a kitchen sink in there? Are you looking over at BSD coreutils in lust? Are you on archlinux? If the answer is yes to all 3 you're in luck, [here ya go], just untar, run the fetch-coreutils.sh script and then simply `makepkg -si`, it packages itself nicely and makes sure it's easily replaceable and uninstallable. NOTICE: This uses [this repo] as a nice way to have all the BSD src code in one place, go thank that guy too!
